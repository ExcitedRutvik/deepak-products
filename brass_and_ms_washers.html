Brass & MS Washers
Manufacturer in Jamnagar

Product Overview

Deepak Products manufactures punched and machined washers in brass and mild steel. Our washers meet DIN, Indian Standards (I.S.), Japanese Standards (J.S.), British Standards (B.S.), and American Standards, ensuring compatibility for global applications.

Special washers can be manufactured as per I.D., O.D. and thickness requirement, as per your drawing or as per the sample you provide. Hardening and tempering options are available for applications requiring enhanced strength.
