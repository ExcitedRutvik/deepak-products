Brass Moulding Inserts
Manufacturer in Jamnagar

Product Overview

Deepak Products manufactures a wide range of Brass Moulding Inserts including threaded moulding inserts, ultrasonic moulding inserts, and knurled moulding inserts. Designed to be molded directly into plastic, wood, or other moulded components, our inserts provide strong, reusable threaded connections that resist pull-out under load.

All threads are manufactured to International Go & Not Go Standards. Deep groove design maximizes pull-out resistance. Available in diamond, straight, and unidirectional knurling patterns, in blind and through variants, round knurled, hexagonal, and square forms, from M2 thread size upward.

Specifications
Material (Brass)CuZn40, CuZn37, CuZn30, CuZn20
Thread StandardInternational Go & Not Go Standards · M2 thread size upward
Groove DesignDeep grooves for maximum pull-out resistance
Knurling TypesDiamond · Straight · Unidirectional
